About Us

Founded in 2010, Helping Hands is a non-profit NGO working in urban and rural areas to provide access to education, healthcare, and basic human rights. Our volunteers are the backbone of our mission.

We run programs for school supplies, skill development workshops, and women empowerment. Our mission is to create a sustainable and compassionate future for all.
